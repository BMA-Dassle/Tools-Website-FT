Racing? Here's What Happens Next

The Racer's Journey

1. Check In at Your Scheduled Time

Your ticket time = check-in time, NOT race start. Head to the 1st floor karting counter at your listed time.

2. Helmetting & Safety Briefing

Get fitted with your helmet and head sock, then attend the mandatory safety briefing. This takes about 10–15 minutes.

3. The Pit & Kart Assignment

Check the pit board for your starting position. Exit in order — your kart number IS your timing identity. Do not pass under yellow.

4. Race, Qualify, Level Up!

Drive smooth, find the gaps, and put down your fastest lap. Your single best lap counts toward qualification. Most racers qualify in 1–2 heats.

Total time from check-in to end of race: approximately 30–45 minutes.
