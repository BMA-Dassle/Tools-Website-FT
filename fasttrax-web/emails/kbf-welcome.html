How to Book Your Free Lanes

1. Visit HeadPinz.com

Go to headpinz.com/hp/kids-bowl-free and select your center.
2. Sign In

Enter the email you registered with at KidsBowlFree.com. We'll send you a quick verification code.
3. Pick Your Bowlers, Date & Time

Select which kids are bowling, choose a date (Mon–Fri), and pick an available time slot.
4. You're Booked!

You'll get a confirmation email and text with your reservation details. Show up and bowl!

Good to Know

⚠️ One reservation at a time.
Each family can hold one active reservation. Need a different day or time? You can reschedule or cancel your current one first.

🎳 2 free games per bowler per day.
Each registered bowler gets one free session (2 games) per day. If they've already played, they can come back as a paid bowler.
