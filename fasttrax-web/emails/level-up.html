⚠️ Before You Race
You've unlocked a faster speed setting. ^LevelName()$ races are a significant step up from what you've been driving. Not every driver is ready for it — drivers who cannot safely handle the increased speed will be removed from the track.

• Do not drift. Drifting kills your lap times and works against your qualifying effort. Smooth, controlled driving is always faster.

• Use the brake. Higher speed means longer braking zones. Brake before the turn, not during it. Your fastest laps will come from clean entries and smooth exits.

• Respect the track. The same rules apply — no intentional contact, follow flag signals, and pull over safely if you need to stop.
