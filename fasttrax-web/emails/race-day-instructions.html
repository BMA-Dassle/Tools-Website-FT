When You Arrive

1. Head to Guest Services 30 Minutes Early

Go upstairs to Guest Services to complete your reservation. Waivers should be completed before arrival using the link below.

2. Guest Services Reviews & Prints Tickets

We'll verify waivers, confirm your booking, and print your race tickets.

3. Check In Downstairs at Your Scheduled Time

Your scheduled time is your check-in time for karting, not your race start time. Head to the downstairs race check-in counter at your listed time. Plan for about 30–45 minutes from check-in to the end of your race.

4. Race, Qualify, Level Up

Drive smooth, hit your qualifying time, and unlock the next tier. Check your results in the app after each heat — your single best lap counts!

How Karts & Timing Work

📋 Your kart number IS your timing identity. The pit board shows your starting position — exit in order. All timing is tied to your kart number.
🟡 Do not pass under yellow flags. Passing under yellow puts you in the wrong kart — your laps won't count toward qualification.
🏁 Up to 14 other drivers on track. Traffic is part of the challenge. Learning to pass is what separates racers who qualify from those who don't.
📈 Only your single fastest lap counts. One clean lap is all you need. Most racers qualify in 1–2 heats.

⚡ Tips to Qualify Faster
• Smooth is fast. Consistent lines beat raw speed every time.
• Learn to pass. Traffic is part of racing. Find the gap, be patient, make your move.
• Use the app. Check your lap times after each heat — your single best lap counts.
• Most racers qualify in 1–2 heats. It's designed to be achievable, not a barrier.
On-Track Safety
Minor bumping can happen — it's part of the track. Excessive or intentional contact = removal from the race.

Feel uncomfortable? Safely pull to the side, stop, and raise your hand. A track marshal will assist you immediately.
