How FastTrax Racing Works

This Isn't Go-Karts. This Is Real Racing.

At FastTrax, every racer earns their speed. Our progression system is designed to keep racing safe, competitive, and exciting — just like real motorsport. You start in Starter class, prove your skill with qualifying lap times, and unlock faster karts and more challenging tracks as you level up.

This is part of the fun. The progression system means every race matters. Hit your qualifying time and you'll unlock the next tier — faster karts, higher speeds, and bragging rights. Most racers qualify in 1–2 races. Some do it on their very first heat.

Your Racing Journey

Level 1

① Starter

Everyone starts here — no exceptions. Speed-controlled karts, friendly competition, perfect for your first time. Ages 13+ / 59"+

To unlock Intermediate: Lap time of 41s (Blue Track) or 46s (Red Track)

Level 2

② Intermediate

Faster karts, competitive lap tracking, challenging layout. For drivers who've proven their skill. Ages 16+ / 59"+

To unlock Pro: Lap time of 32.5s (Blue Track) or 37s (Red Track)

Level 3

③ Pro

The ultimate test. Fastest karts, precision timing, most demanding track configuration. Only the best make it here.

⚡ Tips to Qualify Faster

•Smooth is fast. Avoid sudden braking and sharp turns. Consistent lines beat raw speed.
•Race off-peak. Weekday mornings and early afternoons have fewer racers on track for cleaner laps.
•Use the app. Check your lap times after each heat in the FastTrax Racing App — your best lap counts toward qualification.
•Most racers qualify in 1–2 heats. It's designed to be achievable, not a barrier.

Before Your First Race

1. Book Online & Arrive 30 Min Early

Reserve your heat online or in the app. Arrive early to check in at Guest Services. First-timers need to sign a waiver and get fitted.

2. Check In at The Pit Gate

Scan your QR code or give your name. You'll get your head sock and be directed to the staging area.

3. Race, Qualify, Level Up

Drive smooth, hit your qualifying time, and unlock the next tier. Check your results in the app after each heat. Your best lap counts!
